LEADERBOARD

The Loyola College Ovations Cultural Competition Leaderboard provides a detailed ranking of the college's departments based on their performance in the annual cultural event, Ovations. This leaderboard highlights the overall scores of each department, reflecting their achievements in various cultural categories such as music, dance, drama, and art. It showcases the departments that have excelled in showcasing creativity, talent, and team spirit throughout the competition. By presenting these scores, the leaderboard celebrates the top-performing departments, fosters a spirit of friendly competition, and motivates all departments to elevate their participation and performance in future cultural events.
