OVATIONS'24 - VORTEX

A vortex (aka suzhal) is typically associated with a funnel shape, often seen in water, that begins at the bottom and broadens as it ascends. In our theme, "Vortex into the New, " focuses on the dynamic shape of the vortex itself which is a point from where ideas emerge.

The broad top of the vortex represents the wide range of elements we plan to incorporate, particularly from various cinematic worlds, cultures and genres.

As these diverse cultural, cinematic, and societal elements converge and funnel through the vortex, they blend and merge, ultimately producing something entirely new and innovative at the base.

The theme encapsulates the idea of convergence and transformation, where a multitude of influences come together to create fresh, groundbreaking outcomes.
